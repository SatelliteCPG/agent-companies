# Monthly Category Performance Review

1. Pull monthly velocity trends for all SKUs in managed categories.
2. Identify underperformers — SKUs below category average velocity for 4+ consecutive weeks.
3. Identify fast movers — SKUs exceeding category average by 2x+ that may warrant expanded facings.
4. Review margin contribution by SKU. Flag any margin erosion vs prior month.
5. Review vendor scorecard data for vendors in managed categories.
6. Prepare assortment change recommendations for the next category review.
7. Share findings with Director of Category Management and Category Analyst.
