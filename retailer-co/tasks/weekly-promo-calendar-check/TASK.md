# Weekly Promo Calendar Check

1. Review this week's live promotions — confirm pricing is in systems, displays are set.
2. Review next 2 weeks — confirm all promotions have vendor funding commitments.
3. Review 4-12 week horizon — identify categories with light promotional coverage.
4. Pull post-promo lift data for promotions that ended in the past week.
5. Flag any unfunded promotional slots that need vendor outreach.
6. Flag any promotions at risk due to vendor compliance issues.
7. Update promotional calendar status and share with Director of Merchandising & Promotions.
