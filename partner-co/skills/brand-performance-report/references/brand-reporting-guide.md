# Brand Performance Reporting Guide

Standards and best practices for monthly brand performance reports delivered to brand principals.

## Report Audience

Brand performance reports are read by:
- **Brand Founders/CEOs** — Want the executive summary and next-month priorities. Care about wins and pipeline growth.
- **Brand VP Sales** — Want the details. Care about specific retailer progress, opportunity stages, and meeting outcomes.
- **Brand Operations** — Care about distribution status, void closures, and deduction recovery.

Tailor the report to the primary reader. If the founder reads it, lead with highlights. If the VP Sales reads it, lead with pipeline details.

## Reporting Cadence

| Report | Frequency | Delivery |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Monthly performance report | 1st of month | Email to brand principal |
| Weekly brand sync prep | Friday | Internal prep document |
| Quarterly business review | End of quarter | Presentation to brand leadership |
| Ad-hoc status update | On request | Email or call prep |

## Metric Definitions

### Pipeline Metrics

- **New opportunities**: Opportunities created during the reporting period with this brand's products
- **Opportunities advanced**: Opportunities that moved from one stage to the next (e.g., Outreach to Meeting Scheduled)
- **Won**: Opportunities marked closed-won — new retail authorizations
- **Lost**: Opportunities marked closed-lost — includes reasons (competitive loss, category full, buyer change, etc.)
- **Active pipeline value**: Sum of estimated value for all open opportunities at period end

### Void Metrics

- **Voids identified**: Product-retailer combinations where authorization exists but stocking does not
- **Voids closed**: Previously identified voids where stocking has been confirmed
- **Void closure rate**: Closed / (Identified + Carryover from prior period)

### Deduction Metrics

- **Deductions received**: Dollar amount of new deductions during the period
- **Disputes filed**: Dollar amount of deductions disputed during the period
- **Recovered**: Dollar amount of disputed deductions successfully recovered
- **Recovery rate**: Recovered / Disputed (target: 60%+ for valid disputes)

## Comparison Framework

Always compare metrics month-over-month:
- **Improving**: Metric is better than prior month AND trending in the right direction over 3 months
- **Stable**: Metric is within 10% of prior month
- **Declining**: Metric is worse than prior month OR trending in the wrong direction over 3 months

Use clear directional language: "Pipeline grew 15% month-over-month" not "Pipeline changed."

## Common Brand Principal Questions

Anticipate and answer these in the report:
1. "How many new retailers did we get into this month?"
2. "What is our total pipeline value?"
3. "Are there any stalled deals I should know about?"
4. "How much was recovered in deductions?"
5. "What should we focus on next month?"

## Report Quality Checklist

- [ ] All numbers sourced from CRM data (not estimated)
- [ ] Month-over-month comparison included
- [ ] Next-month priorities are specific (retailer + product + action)
- [ ] Distribution status included with any changes noted
- [ ] Report length is one page equivalent
- [ ] No jargon the brand principal wouldn't understand
- [ ] Delivery within 3 business days of month-end
