# Partner Pipeline Thresholds

Stall thresholds and health benchmarks for a multi-brand broker pipeline. These are tuned for CPG broker sales cycles, which vary by retailer and brand maturity.

## Stall Thresholds by Stage

| Stage | Expected Duration | Stall Threshold | Escalation Action |
|-------|------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Prospecting | 1-2 weeks | 21 days | Re-evaluate retailer fit. Reassign to different rep if needed. |
| Outreach | 1-3 weeks | 21 days | Try alternate contact or different channel (broker referral, trade show follow-up). |
| Meeting Scheduled | 1-2 weeks | 14 days | Confirm meeting is still on calendar. Re-send materials. |
| Presented | 2-4 weeks | 30 days | Follow up within 48 hours of meeting. If 30 days, escalate to VP Sales. |
| Under Review | 2-8 weeks | 45 days | Check if review is tied to category review window. If not, escalate. |
| Category Review | Varies by retailer | 60 days | Tied to retailer review calendar. Only escalate if past known window. |
| Authorized | 1-2 weeks | 21 days | Hand off to operations for DC setup. Flag if setup not initiated. |

## Multi-Brand Considerations

### Brand Portfolio Health

- **Healthy**: 70%+ of brands have at least one active opportunity
- **Warning**: 50-70% of brands have active pipeline
- **Critical**: Below 50% — indicates portfolio stagnation or rep underperformance

### Retailer Coverage Health

- **Healthy**: Average retailer carries 60%+ of relevant portfolio brands
- **Warning**: 40-60% average coverage
- **Critical**: Below 40% — significant cross-sell opportunity being missed

### Pipeline Velocity by Brand Maturity

| Brand Maturity | Expected Close Cycle | Notes |
|---------------|---------------------|-------|
| New to broker (0-6 months) | 60-90 days | Longer cycle expected during relationship building |
| Established (6-24 months) | 30-60 days | Should be at standard velocity |
| Mature (24+ months) | 14-30 days | Mostly reorders and expansions — should be fast |

### Seasonal Adjustments

CPG pipeline is seasonal. Expect:
- **Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: High activity — new year resets, category reviews begin
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: Moderate — executing on Q1 wins, summer promo planning
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Lower — summer slowdown, holiday planning begins
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: High — holiday execution, Q1 planning for next year

Stall thresholds should be relaxed by 50% during Q3 summer slowdown.

## Rep Productivity Benchmarks

| Metric | Target | Warning |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Buyer meetings per week | 3-5 | Below 2 |
| Opportunities created per week | 2-4 | Below 1 |
| Opportunities advanced per week | 3-5 | Below 2 |
| Brands actively pitched per month | 80%+ of portfolio | Below 60% |
