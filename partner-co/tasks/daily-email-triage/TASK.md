# Daily Email Triage

1. Scan all inbound emails since last triage — buyer inquiries, brand principal requests, distributor notices, retailer communications.
2. Route buyer inquiries to the appropriate sales rep or territory manager.
3. Route brand principal requests to VP Client Services.
4. Route distributor and deduction notices to Director of Operations.
5. Create or update contacts and accounts in CRM for all new entities.
6. Log all activities with timestamps, disposition, and brand association.
7. Flag any urgent items — deduction deadlines, category review deadlines, buyer meeting confirmations.
