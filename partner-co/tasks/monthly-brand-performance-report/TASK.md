# Monthly Brand Performance Report

1. For each brand in the portfolio, run brand-performance-report skill.
2. Compile pipeline activity: opportunities created, advanced, won, and lost during the month.
3. List new retail authorizations gained with retailer name, product, and expected first-ship date.
4. Summarize void closures: voids identified and closed during the month by retailer and product.
5. Summarize deduction recovery: disputes filed, resolved, and amounts recovered by retailer.
6. Count buyer meetings held and note key outcomes.
7. Summarize category review submissions and outcomes.
8. Outline top 3-5 priorities for the coming month.
9. Submit reports to VP Client Services for review and approval before distribution to brand principals.
