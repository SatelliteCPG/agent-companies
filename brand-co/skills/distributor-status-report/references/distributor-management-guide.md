# Distributor Management Guide

Reference document for CPG brand teams managing distributor relationships, primarily UNFI and KeHE.

## UNFI DC Structure

UNFI (United Natural Foods Inc.) operates a network of distribution centers organized by region. Key concepts:

### DC Regions
- **East**: Lancaster PA, Dayville CT, Chesterfield NH, Sarasota FL
- **Central**: Greenwood IN, Racine WI, Iowa City IA, Austin TX
- **West**: Moreno Valley CA, Ridgefield WA, Aurora CO, Sacramento CA

### Authorization Levels
- **National**: Product authorized at all DCs. Requires central authorization approval.
- **Regional**: Product authorized at specific DCs. Can be set up DC-by-DC.
- **DC-specific**: Single DC authorization. Typical starting point for emerging brands.

### Code Types
- **Warehouse code**: Product stocked in DC inventory. Standard fulfillment.
- **Direct-store-delivery (DSD)**: Product shipped from brand to retailer, billed through UNFI. Less common for natural/organic.
- **Special order**: Available on request but not stocked. Low volume or niche items.

## KeHE DC Structure

KeHE Distributors operates similarly but with some differences:

### DC Regions
- **East**: Flower Mound TX, Romeoville IL, Harrisburg PA
- **Southeast**: Jacksonville FL, Lithia Springs GA
- **West**: Riverside CA, Portland OR
- **Central**: Dallas TX, Denver CO

### Authorization Process
- New vendor onboarding through KeHE CONNECT portal
- Category review driven — new items reviewed quarterly
- Existing vendors can add SKUs on rolling basis at authorized DCs

## Coverage Metrics

### What to Track

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| DC coverage rate | % of relevant DCs carrying the product | > 80% for established items |
| SKU depth | Average SKUs per DC | All active SKUs at each authorized DC |
| Fill rate | Orders fulfilled vs. orders placed | > 97% |
| Code activation rate | % of submitted codes that are active | 100% within 30 days |
| Regional coverage | % of target regions with at least 1 DC | 100% for national brands |

### Fill Rate Benchmarks

Industry benchmarks for natural/organic CPG:

| Performance Level | Fill Rate | Implication |
|-------------------|-----------|-------------|
| Excellent | > 98.5% | Reliable supplier, preferred status |
| Good | 97-98.5% | Meets expectations |
| Needs improvement | 95-97% | Risk of retailer complaints |
| Critical | < 95% | Risk of discontinuation |

Note: Fill rate data is not currently available in Satellite CRM. This is tracked in distributor portals (UNFI Community, KeHE CONNECT). Flagged as a gap in the synthesis.

## Authorization Workflow

### New Product Authorization

1. **Submit item setup form** with product details, pricing, UPC, case pack, certifications
2. **Distributor reviews** — typically 1-2 weeks for existing vendors, 4-6 weeks for new vendors
3. **Code assigned** — warehouse code generated, shows as "pending" until inventory received
4. **Initial PO** — distributor places first purchase order
5. **Code active** — inventory received and code goes live at the DC

### Common Issues

- **Code pending > 30 days**: Follow up with distributor buyer. May be stuck in approval queue.
- **Code inactive**: Product was authorized but inventory dropped to zero. May need reactivation.
- **Regional vs. national mismatch**: Product authorized nationally but individual DCs haven't activated. Follow up with DC operations.
- **Pricing discrepancies**: Cost submitted to distributor doesn't match what retailers see. Verify through distributor portal.

## Retailer-DC Mapping

Understanding which DCs serve which retailers is critical for distribution gap analysis:

- Large retailers (Kroger, Whole Foods, Sprouts) are typically served by specific DCs based on store location
- A buyer may authorize a product, but if the serving DC doesn't carry it, the product won't reach shelves
- Always verify DC authorization matches the retailer's supply chain before buyer meetings

Note: A comprehensive DC-to-retailer mapping is retailer-specific and changes. The brand team should maintain a working map for their top 10-20 accounts.
