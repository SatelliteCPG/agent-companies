# Pipeline Review Framework

Reference document for pipeline health analysis. Defines stages, expected durations, stall indicators, and action triggers for CPG brand sales pipelines.

## Pipeline Stages

Standard CPG brand pipeline stages from initial contact through authorization:

### 1. Prospecting
- **Definition**: Initial outreach or inbound interest. No formal meeting yet.
- **Expected duration**: 1-2 weeks
- **Stall threshold**: 21 days
- **Healthy signals**: Email exchange, intro call scheduled, broker making introduction
- **Stall signals**: No response to outreach, no meeting scheduled

### 2. Qualification
- **Definition**: First meeting held. Buyer has expressed interest. Evaluating fit.
- **Expected duration**: 2-4 weeks
- **Stall threshold**: 30 days
- **Healthy signals**: Samples requested, sellsheet reviewed, buyer asking pricing questions
- **Stall signals**: Samples not requested, buyer non-responsive after initial meeting

### 3. Proposal
- **Definition**: Formal product proposal submitted. Pricing and terms shared.
- **Expected duration**: 2-4 weeks
- **Stall threshold**: 30 days
- **Healthy signals**: Buyer reviewing pricing, negotiating terms, requesting additional info
- **Stall signals**: No feedback on proposal, buyer "still reviewing" past 3 weeks

### 4. Negotiation
- **Definition**: Active discussion on pricing, placement, promotional support.
- **Expected duration**: 1-3 weeks
- **Stall threshold**: 21 days
- **Healthy signals**: Back-and-forth on terms, specific placement discussed, launch timeline forming
- **Stall signals**: Silence after last counter-offer, "need to check with my team" with no follow-up

### 5. Category Review
- **Definition**: Product submitted for retailer's formal category review process.
- **Expected duration**: Varies widely (4-16 weeks depending on retailer)
- **Stall threshold**: 60 days (unless retailer review window is known)
- **Healthy signals**: Submission confirmed received, follow-up scheduled post-review
- **Stall signals**: No confirmation of receipt, review window passed without update

### 6. Won — Authorized
- **Definition**: Buyer has approved the product. Awaiting DC setup and first shipment.
- **Terminal stage**: Monitor for distribution setup completion

### 7. Lost
- **Definition**: Buyer declined. Record reason for loss.
- **Terminal stage**: Review for re-engagement in next review cycle

## Velocity Metrics

### Benchmark Ranges (CPG Natural/Organic)

These are approximate ranges. Actual velocity depends on retailer size, category, and seasonality.

| Metric | Good | Average | Concerning |
|--------|------|---------|------------|
| Prospecting → Qualification | < 14 days | 14-28 days | > 28 days |
| Qualification → Proposal | < 21 days | 21-42 days | > 42 days |
| Proposal → Negotiation | < 14 days | 14-28 days | > 28 days |
| Full cycle (Prospecting → Won) | < 60 days | 60-120 days | > 120 days |
| Win rate | > 30% | 15-30% | < 15% |

### Seasonal Considerations

- **Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: Category review season for many retailers. High volume of opportunities in Category Review stage is normal.
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: Sell-in season. Pipeline should be growing. Stalled deals from Q1 reviews need resolution.
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Summer slowdown at some retailers. Longer stall thresholds may be appropriate.
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: Holiday promotional focus. New item submissions slow down. Focus on existing authorizations.

## Action Triggers

### Immediate Action Required
- Opportunity value > $50K and stalled > 14 days
- Buyer contact has gone unresponsive (2+ unanswered emails)
- Category review deadline approaching within 7 days
- Competitor won a placement at an account with open opportunities

### This-Week Action
- Any opportunity stalled past its stage threshold
- Account with no meeting in 30+ days and open opportunities
- Samples requested but not shipped within 5 business days
- Sellsheet flagged as outdated for a product in active sell-in

### Monitor / Low Priority
- Opportunities in early stages (Prospecting) with recent activity
- Accounts with no open opportunities but no strategic priority
- Opportunities in Category Review within normal review window
