# Daily Pipeline Check

1. Review all open opportunities for staleness (no activity in 7+ days).
2. Check upcoming meetings in the next 48 hours — ensure briefs are prepared.
3. Identify missing follow-ups from completed meetings.
4. Flag at-risk deals to vp-sales with reason and recommended action.
5. Update pipeline stage and close dates where needed.
