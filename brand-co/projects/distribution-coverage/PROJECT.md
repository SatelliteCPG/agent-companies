# Distribution Coverage

Ensures the distribution network supports the sales pipeline. A retailer cannot stock a product that is not authorized at their serving DC. This project tracks authorization status, identifies gaps, and coordinates new DC setups.

## Key Activities

- Weekly distribution gap analysis (authorized vs. pipeline cross-reference)
- DC item code verification for active sell-in targets
- New DC authorization tracking and setup coordination
- Distributor contact relationship management
- Product family coverage mapping across distribution networks

## Success Metrics

- Percentage of pipeline opportunities with confirmed DC authorization
- Time from authorization request to active DC setup
- Distribution coverage by region (DCs serving active retail targets)
- Gap closure rate (identified gaps resolved per month)
