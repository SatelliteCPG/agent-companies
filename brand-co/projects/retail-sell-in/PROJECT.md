# Retail Sell-In

The primary revenue-generating workflow for the brand. Encompasses everything from initial email contact with a retail buyer through opportunity creation, meeting preparation, sample fulfillment, and deal close.

## Key Activities

- Daily email triage and CRM capture
- Buyer meeting brief preparation
- Pipeline stage advancement and tracking
- Sample request processing and follow-up
- Broker coordination for sell-in support

## Success Metrics

- Pipeline velocity (days per stage)
- Win rate by retailer tier
- Email-to-opportunity conversion rate
- Meeting-to-advancement ratio
