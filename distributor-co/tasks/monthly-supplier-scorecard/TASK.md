# Monthly Supplier Scorecard

1. Pull performance data for all active suppliers from ERP/WMS systems.
2. Calculate composite scores using weighted formula (fill rate 30%, OTIF 25%, shelf-life 20%, quality 15%, responsiveness 10%).
3. Assign tier for each supplier: Platinum (90-100), Gold (80-89), Silver (70-79), Bronze (<70).
4. Compare to prior month. Flag any supplier that dropped a tier.
5. Generate individual scorecards for top 100 suppliers and all suppliers below Gold tier.
6. Identify suppliers below Bronze for 2+ consecutive months — recommend termination review.
7. Deliver scorecard summary with tier distribution to Director of Supplier Relations.
