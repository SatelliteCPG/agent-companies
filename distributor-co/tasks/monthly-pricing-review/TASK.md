# Monthly Pricing Review

1. Review all pending supplier cost changes. Load approved changes with effective dates.
2. Reconcile promotional billbacks from the prior month — calculate amounts owed by each supplier.
3. Generate billback claims and submit to suppliers. Track collection rates.
4. Escalate uncollected billbacks over 60 days to Director of Catalog & Pricing.
5. Analyze margin impact of cost changes by category — flag any categories with margin compression.
6. Identify pricing anomalies: negative margins, below-cost sales, deviated pricing without approval.
7. Report pricing accuracy and margin health to Director of Catalog & Pricing.
