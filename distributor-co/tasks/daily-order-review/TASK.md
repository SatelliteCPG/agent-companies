# Daily Order Review

1. Confirm all incoming purchase orders (EDI 850) were received and acknowledged.
2. Flag orders with issues: invalid item codes, discontinued products, quantity exceptions.
3. Check shipments in transit for today's deliveries. Flag late shipments.
4. Review unresolved retailer inquiries from the prior day.
5. Prioritize open issues by account size and severity.
6. Escalate critical delivery or fulfillment issues to VP Sales/Commercial.
