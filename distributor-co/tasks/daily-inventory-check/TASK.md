# Daily Inventory Check

1. Review overnight fill rates by DC. Flag any facility below 97%.
2. Identify top 5 products driving fill rate shortfalls across the network.
3. Check for warehouse exceptions: mispicks, damaged inventory, receiving discrepancies.
4. Review orders that failed to ship in the prior day. Identify root causes.
5. Check inbound supplier shipments. Flag late arrivals affecting inventory availability.
6. Report critical issues to Director of Supply Chain by 8:00 AM CT.
