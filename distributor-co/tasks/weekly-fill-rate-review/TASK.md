# Weekly Fill Rate Review

1. Compile fill rate by DC for the trailing week. Rank DCs from lowest to highest.
2. Identify products with fill rate below 90% at any DC — these are chronic issues.
3. Cross-reference low fill rate products with supplier performance data.
4. Analyze DC-level trends: is fill rate improving or declining over the trailing 4 weeks?
5. Identify root causes for the top 10 fill rate shortfalls (supplier issue, demand spike, allocation gap).
6. Present findings to Director of Supply Chain with recommended corrective actions.
